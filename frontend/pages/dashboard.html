About MindGuard AI

MindGuard AI is an intelligent mental burnout early warning system that uses machine learning and AI to help professionals detect and prevent burnout before it becomes severe.

By analyzing 8 key behavioral indicators — sleep, stress, workload, screen time, physical activity, social interaction, meal quality, and productivity — the system delivers personalized risk assessments and AI-powered wellness guidance.
